Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Learnova values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: We collect your full name, email address, password (securely hashed), bio, location, and timezone settings.

Profile Information: We store your profile pictures, lists of skills offered, skills wanted, skill descriptions, and proficiency levels.

Community Content: We store posts, achievement images, comments, likes, reviews, and ratings published by users.

Exchange Data: To facilitate learning exchanges, we store exchange requests, request status, meeting-related information, and request messages.

Communication Data: We store direct messages and notification records. We do not record or store audio/video calls conducted outside the platform.

2. Authentication & Security

Passwords are never stored in plain text and are securely hashed before storage. Authentication is managed securely using JSON Web Tokens (JWT).

3. Media Storage

Profile pictures and community images are securely stored using Cloudinary, a trusted third-party cloud storage provider. These images are used only for displaying user profiles and community posts.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to: create your account, match users based on skills, display public profiles, process exchange requests, enable messaging, send notifications, display community content, calculate ratings, and improve platform performance.

5. Reviews and Ratings

Reviews submitted by users become part of the reviewed user's public profile. Average ratings are calculated from all submitted reviews.

6. Notifications

Learnova stores notification records for exchange requests, likes, comments, reviews, and other platform activities to keep users informed.

7. Data Sharing

Learnova does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertising companies. Information is shared only when necessary to operate the platform or comply with legal obligations.

8. Third-Party Services

Learnova uses trusted third-party services, including Cloudinary for secure storage of uploaded images. These providers only process data necessary for delivering platform functionality.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

Learnova may use browser storage technologies (such as Local Storage) to keep users signed in, store authentication tokens, and improve user experience.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information, including password hashing, secure authentication, access controls, and protected database connections. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

11. Data Retention

We retain user information only as long as necessary to operate the platform or comply with legal obligations.

12. Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account. Upon deletion, personal profile information will be removed. Certain community content may remain in anonymized form where necessary to preserve discussions or platform integrity.

13. Children's Privacy

Learnova is not intended for children under the minimum legal age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Learnova after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

15. Contact

If you have questions regarding these Terms or Privacy Policy, please contact the Learnova development team through the contact information provided within the application.
